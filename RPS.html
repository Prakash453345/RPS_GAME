🕹️ How to Play

  1. Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  2. The computer randomly selects its choice.
  3. The winner is decided based on classic RPS rules:

🔥 Rules

  • 5 Rounds
  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Same choice = It’s a tie!

🏆 Winning

  • Win a round = +1 point
  • Track your score, streak, and bragging rights 😎
